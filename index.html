Welcome To HeavenWorld Church

HeavenWorld Church seeks to create a spiritual hospital where the sick, the depressed, the oppressed, and the confused can receive emotional and physical healing, and experience empowerment in the right spiritual environment for the fulfillment of their gifts, talents and assignments in life.

WHO WE ARE

HeavenWorld Church has a divine mandate to raise Solution Providers in the local and international communities that will redefine our moral values and impact our social, political and financial sectors productively through a spiritual relationship with God. Our relationship with God is not based on the works of the law but through the grace that comes from the finished work of Christ. We are the Grace Generation that believes that true transformation of our world can only come through the regeneration of the human soul by the preaching of the glorious good news of the death, burial and resurrection of Jesus Christ.

7 Commitments Of A HeavenWorld Citizen

  • I am committed to be a testimony to the glory of my Lord and Saviour Jesus Christ and to be a good ambassador of HeavenWorld Church. 1 Timothy 4:12-16
  • I am committed to attend church services faithfully and to serve with my time, gifts & talents in a HeavenWorld unit with love for one another & respect for those in authority. Hebrews 10:24-25
  • 1 am committed to winning 12 souls every year & watering    (follow up) them unto spiritual maturity in this church. Proverbs 11:25 & 30; 1 Corinthian 3:6
  • I am committed to reading the entire Bible once every year and other Christian books as may be recommended by the church leadership. Joshua 1:8; 2 Timothy 2:15
  • I am committed to paying my tithes in HeavenWorld and investing in other kingdom projects for the greater influence of the gospel and the growth of the church. Malachi 3:10;    1 Corinthian 16:2. What is Code Editor?
  • I am committed to honoring my relationships, especially with my spouse in the fear of the Lord. Ephesian 5:3; 1 Peter 5:5
  • I am committed to financial integrity in my business and to serve my customers with excellence. Ephesians 4:28; Matthew 5:41

ABOUT OUR SENIOR PASTOR

With over two decades of an excellent life of grace and faith, Victor Orji is today a visionary leader and a prophetic voice who serves as the senior pastor of HeavenWorld Church International, a growing multi-dimensional ministry that is dedicated to the gospel of grace and raising Solution Providers in the local and international communities.
A highly sought-after speaker, whose messages have sparked spiritual, social and financial revolution in the lives of many at several church meetings and public conferences around the world.
He is the executive producer of 'Everyday Success', a special TV and Radio program that has blessed many homes.
He is also a prolific writer and the editor of WonderNews, a free monthly magazine of HeavenWorld Church that is glorifying the present-day ministry of Jesus and edifying people towards a grace lifestyle.

He is happily married to his lovely wife, Pastor Ezinne, and they are blessed with four children, Deborah, Esther, Victoria and Victor.

About Us

A church is a group of people who are, in a word, together. We’re together with God because of his kindness and commitment to humanity; together as a family learning to embody this kindness and commitment one to another and beyond into the wider world. All this is only possible because of Jesus, the one who brings us together and keeps us together.

Heaven World Church is a community of Christians who gather and serve in Fort Langley, Willoughby and their surrounding neighborhoods. We meet each Sunday, connect during the week in Life Groups, and serve in the community all over.

We are guided by seven central values. They reflect both who we are and who we hope to be. For more on the values, click here.

Accepting Jesus Christ

To receive Jesus Christ as the Lord and Savior of your life today, sincerely pray this prayer from your heart.
Dear Jesus, I am sorry for my sins and I ask You to forgive me now. I believe that You died for me and that You rose again on the third day. I confess to You that I am a sinner and that I need Your love and forgiveness. Cleanse my heart with your blood. Come into my life, forgive my sins, and give me eternal life. I confess You now as my Lord. Thank You for my Salvation.
If you prayed that prayer of salvation we want you to know that God has brought you back into a loving relationship with Him through His Son Jesus Christ. You are forgiven. You have salvation. You are blessed.
